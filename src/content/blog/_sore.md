# News: Wavey Dynamics at the M.I.A. School of Race Engineering

We're tutoring at the [M.I.A. School of Race Engineering](https://www.schoolofraceengineering.co.uk/) this Autumn!

![](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/67d9bf_9da6c086c3154ee68b3ce07792c17c54~mv2.jpg)

Alongside leading names in trackside engineering with backgrounds from F1, BTCC, Formula E and WEC, we'll be teaching the younger generation of aspiring race and trackside engineers some of what they need to know to prepare for their career.

The School of Race Engineering has been running for a couple of years now and makes strong inroads into opening motorsport engineering up to a wider audience by allowing students to learn from and network with some established and experienced motorsport engineers in a great environment.

As part of the four day course, our director, Jahee will be giving the lecture covering motorsport suspension technology on October the 31st.

Jahee had the following to say;

> This is a great opportunity to continue our effort into giving back and helping the younger generation of motorsport engineers with some knowledge and understanding gained over my time within motorsport engineering.

> It's nice to be recognised by a long established and reputable association such as the M.I.A. and i'm looking forward to meeting the students and being involved.

Hope to see some of you guys there!
